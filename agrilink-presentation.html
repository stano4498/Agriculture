AgriLink Smart Farming Hub

By Stanley Mutune

What is the Project?

AgriLink is a web-based platform that helps farmers access smart farming tools, data, and expert knowledge.

Why Did I Choose the Idea?

To help small-scale farmers overcome challenges like lack of information, tools, and market access.

What Problem Does It Solve?

It solves poor access to farming knowledge, lack of smart tools, poor market linkages, and weather-related crop loss.

Who Is It Meant For?

Farmers, agricultural officers, agritech startups, and consumers seeking fresh local produce.

Tech Stack Used

HTML, CSS, Google Maps Embed API, Responsive Design

Key Features

Responsive design, product listings, smart tips, contact form, Google Maps embed, clean UI.

Challenges Faced

Embedding maps, icon display issues, mobile responsiveness, generating meaningful content.

How I Solved Them

Used correct embed codes, loaded icons via CDN, styled with media queries, and researched content.

Future Improvements

User login, real-time weather API, chatbot support, e-commerce tools, multilingual support.
